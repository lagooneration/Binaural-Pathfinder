Scope of the Research

  • Model a listener (subject) moving in a 3D environment from point A to point B.
  • Place two speakers at different positions; simulate binaural intensity fields.
  • Use evolutionary strategies to discover efficient paths that maximize hearing quality while minimizing travel cost.
  • Visualize learning progress across generations and the resulting optimized routes.

Concept Visualization

The ground plane encodes a sound field: red indicates right-speaker dominance, blue indicates left-speaker dominance, and purple indicates balance. The evolving subject explores different vector paths; the best paths are drawn with vertex colors that reflect which ear receives stronger signal along the route. Pulsing rings around speakers convey sound emission.

Method at a Glance

  1. Represent a path as control points between A and B; interpolate with a smooth curve.
  2. Define a fitness that combines average binaural intensity with path efficiency.
  3. Evolve a population with selection, crossover, and mutation over multiple generations.
  4. Animate top candidates; compare how they leverage the sound field to reach B.
