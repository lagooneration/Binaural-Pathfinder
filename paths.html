2D Paths — Concept

A minimal SVG animation that illustrates multiple candidate paths from A to B; the algorithm highlights the best one repeatedly.

Outcomes

  • Animated exploration of paths between two points in a field.
  • Highlighting the optimized path based on a simple cost function.
  • Intuitive understanding of pathfinding and trade-offs.

What’s happening

  • Paths are generated and scored using distance and loudness balance.
  • Top path is emphasized; others fade to show comparison.
  • Looping keeps focus on the best solution.
